The text is a transcript of Nvidia's Q1 2024 earnings call. The company reported strong demand for its high-performance networking platforms, particularly in the areas of generative AI and accelerated computing. The gross margins for the quarter were reported as 64.6% on a GAAP basis and 66.8% on a non-GAAP basis. Nvidia expects future growth primarily driven by increased demand for data center solutions related to generative AI and large language models. The company also highlighted its focus on developing software and infrastructure to support customers in the field of AI, including the importance of Nvidia AI Enterprise, its operating system for AI.
